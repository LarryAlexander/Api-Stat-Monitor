# PulseBoard Open Core Strategy

## Recommendation

Use an `open core + paid hosted` model.

## What should likely be open

- core dashboard app
- local setup
- monitor CRUD
- local check execution
- basic incident tracking
- self-hosting instructions

## What should likely remain paid

- hosted infrastructure
- recurring managed scheduling
- alert delivery
- premium integrations
- analytics and reporting
- team features
- reliability guarantees
- support

## Why this model fits PulseBoard

- it builds trust
- it helps portfolio and developer adoption
- it still leaves a clear paid convenience layer
- it avoids trying to outcompete mature closed products with a fully closed early SaaS

## Do not do yet

- do not publish open source until setup and docs are clean
- do not launch paid until the recurring check and alert system exists
